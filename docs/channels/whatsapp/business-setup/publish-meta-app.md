# Publish Meta App
**Estimated Time: 1 minute**

Before you can send WhatsApp messages to customers, your Meta app must be published.

:::danger Final Step Before Sending Messages
If your app remains in **Development** mode, you cannot message your bot.
:::

## Publish your app
1. Visit [Meta Developers](https://developers.facebook.com/apps) and open your app.
2. On the app dashboard, find **Publish** on the left hand side bar.
3. Click **Publish**, you may need to add your privacy url and terms of service url.
4. Confirm the publish action.

## Verify everything is ready
- Confirm your webhook is verified in Meta.
- Confirm the **messages** webhook field is subscribed.
- Send a test message from your WhatsApp Business number.
