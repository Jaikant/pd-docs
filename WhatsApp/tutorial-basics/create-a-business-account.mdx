# Create a Business Account
**Estimated Time: 2 minutes**

1. **Visit the Facebook Business Portal**
   - Open your web browser and go to [Facebook Business](https://business.facebook.com). If you are not already logged in, enter your Facebook credentials to log in.

2. **Initiate Business Account Setup**
   - Look for the **Create a business portfolio** option, which you can find on clicking the dropdown on the left sidebar of the homepage. Click on this link to begin setting up your business account.
   - Complete the form with the required details to create your business account. Once you click on create, you may see several dialogs offering to add assets or users. You can skip these.
